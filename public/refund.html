Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with 0machine. This Refund Policy explains when you are eligible for a refund and how to request one.

📌 In summary: Free trial → no charge. Monthly plans → no refund. Annual plans → pro-rata refund within 30 days of renewal. Service issues → always contactus and we'll make it right.

1. Free Trial

0machine offers a 3-day free trial with no credit card required. You will not be charged during the trial period. If you do not wish to continue, simply let the trial expire or cancel from your account settings — no action needed and no charge will apply.

2. Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions are prepaid and non-refundable once the billing period has started. If you cancel a monthly subscription, you will retain access to the Service until the end of the current billing period, after which no further charges will occur.

3. Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscriptions, we offer a pro-rata refund on the unused portion of your plan if you cancel within 30 days of your annual renewal date. After 30 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable for the remainder of the period.

Example: If you are billed annually at $90 on 1 January and cancel on 15 January (14 days after renewal), you can request a refund for approximately 11.5 months of unused service (≈$86).

4. Service Disruptions

If you experience extended service outages that are directly caused by our infrastructure failures and lasting more than 24 continuous hours, you may be eligible for service credits. Please contact us at support@0machine.com with details.

5. Duplicate Charges

If you believe you have been charged in error or in duplicate, contact us at billing@0machine.com within 7 days of the charge. We will investigate and process a full refund of any erroneous charge within 5-10 business days.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email billing@0machine.com
  • Include your account email and a description of your request.
  • We will respond within 2 business days.
  • Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

7. Exclusions

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Failure to cancel a subscription before the renewal date.
  • Requests made after the applicable refund window has passed.
  • Accounts suspended or terminated due to violations of our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy.
  • Third-party fees charged by payment processors or banks.

8. Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy limits your statutory consumer rights under applicable law, including rights under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 (UK) or equivalent EU consumer protection law.
