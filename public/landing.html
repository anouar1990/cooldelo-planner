Features

Everything for your fabrication workflow

Tailored for laser cutting, CNC, and fabrication professionals. Stop using spreadsheets—start using tools built for your shop.

🧮
Real-Time Cost Calculator
Accurately price every job with material cost, laser time, electricity, machine wear, labor, and profit margin — updated instantly as you type.
📦
Material Inventory
Track all your sheets, panels, and rolls. Monitor stock levels, unit costs, and total inventory value. Low-stock alerts included.
📋
Orders Management
Manage jobs from Pending → In Progress → Completed. Track revenue per client, deadlines, and order history in one place.
⚡
Laser Presets Library
Save your perfect speed, power, frequency, and pass settings for every material. Duplicate presets, star favorites, and never lose settings again.
📄
Client Quote Generator
Build professional quotes with multiple line items, VAT, validity dates, and status tracking (Draft → Sent → Accepted). Ready to share in seconds.
🔲
Nesting Estimator
Calculate how many parts fit on a sheet. Supports rotation optimization, kerf, margins. Shows material efficiency %, waste %, and sheets needed.
